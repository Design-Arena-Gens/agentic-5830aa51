Definition

\[ f(x) = (\pi - \tfrac{x}{2})^2,\quad 0 \le x \le 2\pi,\quad f(x+2\pi)=f(x). \]

Fourier Series Expansion

\[ f(x) = \frac{\pi^2}{3} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \left( \frac{1}{n^2}\cos(nx) + \frac{\pi}{n}\sin(nx) \right). \]
